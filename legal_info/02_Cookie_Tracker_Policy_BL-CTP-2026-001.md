## 1. Introduction

This Cookie & Tracker Policy explains how Betopia Limited uses cookies, web beacons, tracking pixels, and similar technologies on betopiagroup.com and betopialimited.com. We will always ask for your consent before placing non-essential cookies on your device.

---

## 2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. We also use:
- **Web beacons / tracking pixels** — tiny invisible images that register when a page or email is opened
- **JavaScript tags** — code snippets (via GTM) that enable analytics and marketing tracking
- **Session/local storage** — browser storage for functionality and session management
- **Device fingerprinting** — Hotjar uses anonymised technical attributes to identify returning visitors

## 5. Google Tag Manager

We use GTM to manage and deploy all cookies listed above. GTM is configured with **Consent Mode v2** so that tags only fire for the categories of cookies you have consented to. Marketing tags (Meta Pixel, LinkedIn Insight Tag) will not fire unless you have consented to marketing cookies.

## 6. Your Cookie Choices

### 6.1 Cookie Consent Banner
When you first visit our websites, you may:
- **Accept all cookies** — consent to all four categories
- **Reject non-essential cookies** — only strictly necessary cookies will be placed
- **Customise preferences** — select specific categories via the Cookie Preference Centre

You can change your preferences at any time via 'Cookie Settings' in our website footer.

### 6.2 Browser Controls
Most browsers allow you to refuse new cookies, accept or reject specific cookies, and delete existing cookies:
- **Chrome:** Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
- **Firefox:** Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari:** Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
- **Edge:** Settings > Cookies and site permissions

### 6.3 Tool-Specific Opt-Out Links

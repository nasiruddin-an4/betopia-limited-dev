## 11. Information Security

Betopia is aligned with ISO 27001 and holds ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 22301:2019 certifications. Key technical and organisational measures include:
- TLS 1.2+ encryption in transit; AES-256 encryption at rest
- Role-based access control (RBAC) and least-privilege principles
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) for all administrative access
- Regular vulnerability scanning and annual penetration testing
- Annual mandatory data protection training for all staff
- Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) with all sub-processors
- 72-hour breach notification to supervisory authorities (GDPR Article 33)

## 12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA / CPRA)

California residents have rights to know, delete, correct, opt-out of sharing, and non-discrimination. Note: Betopia does not sell personal information for monetary consideration. Sharing data with advertising platforms (Meta, LinkedIn) for targeted advertising may constitute 'sharing' under CPRA. To submit a CCPA request, email **privacy@betopialimited.com** with subject 'CCPA Request'.
